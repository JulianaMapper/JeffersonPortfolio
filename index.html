Who I Am

The through line.

My career is one story: I find the place where a complex system fails a human being, and then I build something to fix it. It started in aviation — studying how machines fail the people who operate them. It runs through pediatric burn data at Children's National, COVID vaccine equity at Lake County Health Department, and a 7-layer AI platform I personally architected on AWS.

I am currently CEO & Co-Founder of Flourish & Thrive Labs, where we build public health data infrastructure for the organizations that can't afford to wait for enterprise vendors. I am also a DrPH candidate at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health.

I'm open to executive leadership opportunities in health IT, public health informatics, and AI infrastructure. If your organization is at an inflection point — a major technology transformation, a platform that needs to actually get built, or a team that needs a leader who can speak both the strategy and the architecture — I'd like to talk.
